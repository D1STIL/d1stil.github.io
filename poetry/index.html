Dewan Mukto's Poetry


Nocturnum

A poem to appreciate the beauty of night time creeping close to souls.

Wintry Day

A poem about the coldest season that manages to render the warmest emotions.

Insomnia

A poem about the tough opponent some people have to defeat in order to induce a cozy sleeping state.

Broken

A poem about the harshest experience a person can ever feel in spite of all that they possess.

Handicap

A poem to express the sorrow felt by a person with problems - any and all kinds of dilemmas

Thorns

A metaphoric poem to honor the prickly protectors of flowers.

Sarcasm

A poem to introduce the peculiar thread of emotions next of kin to humor

Headache

A poem to complain about that annoying feeling that bullies some peoples' scalp

Thrones

A poem about the kingly seats of old.

Last Night

An abstract poem about a person's nocturnal adventures

Sniper

A poem to honor the sharpshooters who protect valuable life at the cost of taking others'.

War 4 War

A poem to contemplate on the act of violence that humans are accused of.

Mooncore

A poem to promote Nightcore music.

Hope

A poem to praise a holy instrument for the mind.

Coffee

A poem to praise the caffeinated drink all Americans love.
